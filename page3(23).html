Pros

Aspects that make this credit card amazing.

  1. No expiry of Reward Points.
  2. It offers discounts on dining at selected restaurants.
  3. The card gives reward points, fuel and other spends.
  4. It has zero lost card liability.

Cons

Aspects this credit card lacks.

  1. No concierge services other than card assistance.
  2. Convenience fee of ₹ 99 is levied on the EMI conversions.
  3. The card is available in selected cities only.
  4. Membership points can not be earned on spending on fuel, insurance and utility.
