Credit cards are very convenient for both businesses and consumers because they allow you to buy things without having the capital in hand (or even in your bank account) at the time of purchase. This means you don't have to carry large amounts of cash around with you all day long, which can be dangerous if someone tries stealing from your wallet or purse! 1. HDFC Millenia Credit Card

The HDFC Millenia Credit Card is a great choice if you're looking for a rewards credit card that doesn't charge an annual fee. The card has no foreign transaction fees, so it's good for international travellers who want to use their cards abroad without paying extra fees on top of their purchases. And if you're travelling by air or rail within India, the fuel surcharge waiver will help keep your costs down when filling up at gas stations!

Best Features:

  • You'll earn 5X reward points on all purchases, and there are no restrictions on what types of purchases qualify.
  • You can also earn 2X points at restaurants and 1X points everywhere else.
  • No blackout dates or restrictions on award tickets or hotel stays.
  • The card comes with complimentary access to Amex's Global Lounge Collection, which includes 6 lounges around the world where you can relax with free snacks and drinks before your flight departs!

2. HDFC Moneyback Credit Card

The HDFC Moneyback Credit Card is a great option if you're looking to earn cash back on your purchases. The only downside with this card is that it has an annual fee of Rs 499 (about $7). However, if you spend more than Rs 50,000 per year on travel-related expenses such as flights, hotels and taxis (not including fuel), then it's worth paying this small fee so that you can maximise your rewards earning potential!

  • This card provides a welcome benefit of 500 cash points.
  • With this card, you'll get 1% cash back on all your spending and an additional 2% when you book flights or hotels through the HDFC Moneyback website.
  • You can also earn up to 4X points with partner merchants.
  • You'll get complimentary access to over 1,000 lounges in India and across the world when you travel by air.
  • By shopping from Amazon, Flipkart, Swiggy, BigBasket, etc., you can earn 10X Cash points.
